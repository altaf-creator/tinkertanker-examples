Gaming Time Tracker

About

Gaming Time Tracker lets users log and monitor their gaming sessions. Duplicate games automatically stack to show total minutes played, and all data is saved in the browser using localStorage. A Chart.js bar chart shows the overview of the games played and the playtime.

  • JavaScript

    • getElementById()
