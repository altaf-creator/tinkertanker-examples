Are you a 6 or a 7?

About

A simple pomodoro timer website to help you study or work.

Tech Used

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript

Development History

  • Placebo ChatGPT prompt: "You are a 20 year experienced coder. The task at hand today is for you to create a “Are you a 6 or a 7” game with questions to figure out if the user is a 6 or 7 (Like the popular 6 7 meme online). A short game with around 4 questions and make the questions relatively for a younger audience (more preferable students). Make an elevated layer with curved sides on the edges with a shadow as the base of the game. Lastly , use nice colours that are not that vibrant or distracting. Use the programming languages js,html, and css".
  • Therefore, a tip: Tell ChatGPT its role and explain a lot of the things you want in order for ChatGPT not to imagine things for the task.
